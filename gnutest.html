Introduction

GNU Source Installer (sourceinstall) is a source package installer and manager for Unix-likes. On this page, results of my tests with GNU packages will be displayed.

It is my hope that with time, most GNU packages will make good use of the autotools, for the benefit of users and packagers alike. This will also give a whole gnu sense of cohesion.
